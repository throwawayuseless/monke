A variant on Kakologophobia, which replaces the trauma with a mood debuff.

This variant is programmed similarly to a /datum/brain_trauma/mild/phobia, but is deliberately not implemented in terms of that system, as we only want to give a mood debuff, rather than shutting down the character entirely.
